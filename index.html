Game Highlights

Reverse Boss Battles

Experience the game from the boss's perspective, defending against heroes who challenge your dominion.

Dynamic Narrative

Engage in a compelling story that explores themes of power, persistence, and change, influenced by your choices.

Atmospheric Pixel Art

Immerse yourself in a dark fantasy world brought to life with detailed pixel art and a haunting soundtrack.

Dark Queen's Powers

Shadow Strike

Unleash a swift attack from the shadows, catching heroes off guard and dealing significant damage.

Dark Aura

Emit an aura of darkness that weakens nearby heroes, reducing their attack power and resolve.

Summon Minions

Call forth loyal minions to assist in battle, overwhelming heroes with numbers and strategy.

Teleportation

Instantly relocate within your lair, evading attacks and positioning yourself for a counterstrike.

Life Drain

Drain the life force of heroes to heal your wounds, turning their strength into your advantage.

Fear Induction

Instill fear into the hearts of heroes, causing them to hesitate or flee, giving you the upper hand.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the game?

Click the "Play Now" button, register an account, and immerse yourself in the world of Mortholme.

Is the game free to play?

Yes, Mortholme is completely free, offering all players a full and engaging experience.

Is multiplayer mode available?

Currently, the game focuses on a single-player narrative. Future updates may introduce cooperative multiplayer modes.

Can I save my progress?

Yes, the game automatically saves your progress, allowing you to pick up where you left off.

What platforms are supported?

Mortholme is available on PC and Mac. Mobile support may be introduced in future updates.
